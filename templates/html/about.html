About Us

Getting together with real people in real life makes powerful things happen. Side hustles become careers, ideas become movements, and chance encounters become lifelong connections. Meetup brings people together to create thriving communities. One way to meet new people, to make friends or practice your social skills, is through meet ups organized over the internet. At the moment the most popular and well-known site for this is questioner.com, which lets you attend or create local meet up groups
